Monthly Reports

On the 20th of the month, you will be assigned monthly reports to complete for all of your students. In the menu, the "Monthly Reports" item will display this icon: 

On the schedule page, you will see a box outlined in red titled "Unanswered posts."

Click "Monthly Reports" to go to to Reports page.

Here, you will see a list of all of your students for whom you must write monthly reports. 

To write a report, click the [Evaluation] icon on the far right side of the row. 

Writing Reports

When you click on the [Evaluation] icon, a pop-up box will appear with the student's information from that month.

You must complete the evaluations for each area. Add only one point per month. In the example below, Jin earned an additional point for Vocabulary in the month of July.

In the "Evaluations" box, write one to two sentences for each area. In the "Improvement" box, provide suggestions for your student that they can implement on their own. 

When you are finished, click the drop-down box next to "Results Send" and change it to "Yes." Then click "Save." This report will now appear in the list of "Complete" reports.

Tips for Completing the Monthly Report

Consult the calendar. 

1. Click the [CLASS] icon from the monthly report page (under the "Class Info" column).

2. This will open the student's information for that month. From here you can use the red buttons to click through the reports for each class. You can also click "Class Calendar" to see all classes from that month.

3. Here you will see all of the student's classes for that month. By clicking the red [Contents] icon, you can easily review the information for the class. Click on a few of these to get an overall impression of the student's performance that month.
