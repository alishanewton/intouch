Tag the student's attendance within 3 minutes of ending class.

To the right of the box labeled "Today's Attendance," you will see three options.

1. Ready: This is the default option for each class. If you leave "Ready" selected, you will see a dialog box when you click "Save":

You choose "Ready" for today's attendance. Is it correct?

2. Present: If the student was able to complete at least 30% of the class (50% for CREDU students). When you save the class with the "Present" option selected, you will see a dialog box:

You choose "Present" for today's attendance. Is it correct?

3. Absent: If the student did not answer, answered and asked to cancel, or could not complete at least 30% of the class (50% for CREDU students). When you save the class with the "Absent" option selected, you will see a dialog box:

You choose "Absent" for today's attendance. Is it correct? The absent SMS will be sent automatically.

After you click "OK" on the dialog box, you will see another message in the box:

Click "Close" to exit the student page.
