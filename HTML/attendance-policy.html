Attendance Policy

To report to your shift, open Skype and send a message to Sunny (sunny_kim2). You should do this 10 minutes before the start of your shift.

If you are not able to log in on time, please send a Skype message to Sunny with your expected start time. 

If you are not able to complete your shift due to unavoidable circumstances, you must notify Sunny via call or message to explain the situation.  

Penalty for Missed Classes

Tardiness and absences are subject to salary deduction.
