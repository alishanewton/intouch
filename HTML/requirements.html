Internet Requirements

Avoid sharing the Internet during class. For instance, shared public WiFi networks at coffee shops or libraries may be not have sufficient speed for conducting calls. Or in another case, if someone else in your house is streaming a movie, it will affect the voice quality of your call.  

The minimum upload speed of the Internet is 3 Mbps. Check your upload speed with Google's Internet speed test or Ookla.

You can see the current network connection strength within X-Lite once the call has been established.

The green icon indicates that your network connection is good. If the icon is red, your student may tell you that he or she cannot hear you. 

  

Equipment Requirements

Use a headset for all classes.
