Schedule Page

When you first log in to the Learning Management System (LMS), you will see your daily schedule for the current day in Korea Standard Time (KST) or GMT+9. 

The schedule is organized by time. So your first students of the day are at the top of the page. 

To view your schedule for a different day, change the date by clicking in the date box, selecting the date you want to view, and clicking "Search."

From your schedule, you can click [INFO] to open the class information for that student. If this icon is not present, then the class is not ready or active. 

Weekly Back Up

Back up your schedule once a week for the next week's classes.

1. Choose the start date of the following week and click "Search." This will display the schedule of this day. 

2. Download the weekly schedule by by clicking the "Weekly EXCEL" button.

Backing up your schedule will help you if your Internet is working but the Intouch website fails to load for whatever reason.
