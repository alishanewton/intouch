Comments & Evaluations 

Beneath the student and class information, you will see radio buttons to rate the student's comprehension, fluency, pronunciation, vocabulary, and grammar for that session.

    Evaluations for each class can change on a session-by-session basis.
Below the evaluations section is a series of boxes for entering comments:
  • Pronunciation and Grammar: Errors the student made during the class, with corresponding corrections
  • Pardon: Supplementary explanation or additional comments that the student was not able to understand during class
  • Comment: Overall comments for the class and/or requests to the student

Complete all comments and evaluations within 3 hours of finishing class.
