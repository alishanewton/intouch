Using X-Lite

You will use X-Lite, a Voice-over-IP (VoIP) program, to make calls.

1. Make sure the X-Lite program is open on your computer.

2. Navigate to the student page and click the "Auto Dial" button:

 

3. You will see an Incoming call to X-Lite. Click the green button to answer the call. 

4. Once the call has been established, X-Lite will dial your student's cell phone. 

If the Auto Dial button is not working for whatever reason, you can copy and paste the student's mobile phone number into the dialing box on X-Lite.
