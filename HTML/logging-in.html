Logging In 

To access the login page, go to http://175.126.123.193/_TUTOR/main/login.html.

Your account will be created by Intouch Learning.

When you first login, your username and password will have the following format:

Username: [firstname][1 or 2]usa

Password: qwer12

If you are teaching in the morning and the evening, you will have two different accounts. The accounts are signified by the numbers 1 and/or 2 in the username:

  • 1: morning, 6 to 9:30 AM in Korea Standard Time (KST) or GMT+9
  • 2: evening, 7 to 11 PM Korea Standard Time (KST) or GMT+9

Changing Your Password

After logging in for the first time, change your password by navigating to My Info and entering a new password.
