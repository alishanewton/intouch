The notice board is for recording unusual circumstances.

Click "write" to post a message to the study managers. You can choose from the following categories:

Here are some cases when it is appropriate to write a message:

  • Late call: You called the student more than 2 minutes late.
  • Miss call: You did not call the student at all, for whatever reason. 
  • Change textbook: The student has requested to change their textbook, or there is some other problem with the textbook.
  • Change schedule: The student wants to change their schedule. 
  • Etc: The student does not have a level test. 

There are two ways to send a message.

1. From the Staff notice board: You can write a message by clicking "write." A pop-up box will appear:

2. From the student page: Click "Write Board." This will automatically include the student's name and ID when the pop-up box appears.
